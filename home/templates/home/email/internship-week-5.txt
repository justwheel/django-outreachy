Outreachy week 5: Assignments for mentors & interns

Welcome to week five of your Outreachy internship! We hope you're making good progress on your project. Please reach out to organizers@outreachy.org if you need additional help or advice!

We have some goals for interns. Please read the sections below.

For most interns, feedback #2 is due on {{ current_round.midfeedback }}. The feedback #2 form will be available on {{ current_round.intern_midpoint_feedback_opens }}, at which point you'll see a button on your dashboard:

The goal of feedback #2 is to ensure that interns are working full-time on their projects, communicating with their mentor(s), and revising their contributions based on feedback in a timely manner.

Week 5 Focus
---------------------------

This week is about making sure everyone has a shared understanding of how the project is going.

By now, interns have usually moved past onboarding and into deeper project work. This is a good time to:

- Reconfirm what you are working on

- Check whether timelines or priorities have shifted

- Surface any confusion or blockers

- Make sure feedback is flowing both ways

Again, if you have any questions, please contact us!
